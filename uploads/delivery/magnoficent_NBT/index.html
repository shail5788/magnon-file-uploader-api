Accelerating the partnership between Valvoline & the mechanics’ community
Valvoline, a worldwide brand of premium lubricants, created a dedicated forum “Valvoline Mechanics Week” to honour their relationship with mechanics. To help Valvoline achieve its objective of increasing awareness and engagement with key influencers like mechanics, Magnon\TBWA devised a well-thought-out #ChampionMechanics of South-East Asia campaign.

With over 700 mechanics participating and cumulative reach of 2.6 million, the social media handles were flooded with responses from mechanics from all over South-East Asia. The campaign not only created a buzz for the event but also amplified the brand’s on-ground activities.
Winning the Risland Pitch
